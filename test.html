THE WESTBRIDGE HOTEL

With its intimate atmosphere and classic British charm, we like to think of The Westbridge as a home away from home. A place where professional and friendly staff will be happy to attend to your every need; where attention to detail matters; and where location couldn’t be more prime. It’s not often you’ll find a sophisticated hideaway in the capital’s most innovative area. The Westbridge is located just minutes from the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, Westfield Stratford City, O2 Arena and ExCel London exhibition centre . With this in mind, we’re delighted to welcome back many of our corporate and leisure guests time and time again.
