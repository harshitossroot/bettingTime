Privacy Policy

This privacy statement explains how we collect and utilizes information. This Privacy Policy applies to the "SCAN-IT", Website at http://scanpos.isuf.co.uk/ (the “Site”), and to the applications for Android and IOS platforms "SCAN-IT" (the “Apps”).

Personal Identifiable Information Collection

We do collect, store Personal Identifiable Information. We also collect aggregate information that help us to better understand the client's behavior and consequently to indicate to us where to make improvement of the apps performances.

Use of Non-Personal information and of Aggregate Information

Aggregate information or Non-Personal information could be shared with:

- Third parties that provide services to us to better refine the improvement of the apps or to understand the client's behavior in a clearer way.

- Managers and higher authorities.

Anyway aggregate information will not be disclosed to government institutions or authorities except if required by law.

Changes

We may change this Privacy Policy at any time, at our sole discretion. Use of information we collect now is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is used.

If we make changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you by posting an announcement on the Site so you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances if any, it is disclosed.
